STREET TACOS

Street tacos are delicious, amazing, and oh so mouthwatering! Bite into tender steak, zesty lime flavor with a hint of spice and add on tomatoes, avocado, and onions for a savory bite you are going to love!

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 6 tacos

Ingredients

  • Flank steak
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Lime
  • Minced garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Corn tortillas

Directions

  1. Cut the flank steak into one-inch pieces and mix with seasonings.
  2. Marinate in the fridge for 1-2 hours.
  3. Cook on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Assemble tacos with desired toppings.
